ABOUT US

Lamborghini is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer known for producing high-performance and exotic automobiles. The company was founded by Ferruccio Lamborghini in 1963, and it has its headquarters in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy. Lamborghini cars are renowned for their striking design, powerful engines, and exceptional performance.

HISTORY

Ferruccio Lamborghini, originally a tractor manufacturer, started Lamborghini Automobili S.p.A. to compete with established sports car manufacturers like Ferrari. The company quickly gained a reputation for building high-performance vehicles.

LIMITED PRODUCTION

Lamborghini often produces its cars in limited quantities, adding to their exclusivity and allure among enthusiasts. Special editions and variants are also common.
